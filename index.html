How to use Kraph:

  • double click on empty space: create a new node
  • double click on a node/edge: edit data of node/edge. Clicking out of the window saves the data
  • click on node/edge: selection
  • click on empty space + drag: selection
  • click on empty space: deselection
  • click on selected entity + drag: move
  • ctrl+click on selected entity + drag: create a new node, child of the previous one
  • left or right arrows when edges are selected: toggle plugs on and off
